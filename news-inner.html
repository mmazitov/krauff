NEW SERIES WARE KRAUFF FEINE - elegance in every detail

A new series of tableware

A new series of tableware Krauff Feine - excellent dinner set premium. Krauff Feine - is a shining accessory on your plate and a modern functional tool to help each owner. The first thing that catches the eye when looking at the dishes Krauff Feine - gentle elegant design, radiant glossy finish, coupled with intricate decorations on the outside wall of the pan - this combination creates a beautiful modern design that emphasizes the benefit of its owner's taste.

For the production of tableware series Krauff Feine used steel premium Ecosteel - safe not only for your health but also for the environment because it does not emit harmful substances, even when exposed to high temperatures.

  1. An important advantage of this steel are increased wear resistance and corrosion
  2. Resistance - steel does not tarnish over the years, keeps the great features and quality.
  3. Thanks to a uniform glossy surface, the steel does not absorb the smell of food.
    • The material does not react with acids and alkalis
    • does not oxidize products and preserve their natural flavor and more nutrients than usual pan.

Pan handles

3-ply capsule bottom and sealed walls allow to cook meals faster than a conventional bowl stainless steel - heat quickly and evenly throughout the container protects the food from sticking.

  • Pan handles are also made of steel and are attached to the
  • Ecosteel walls using solid rivets that ensures a secure fit pens for many years.

Saucepan lids are made of heat-resistant glass, which perfectly resists high temperatures up to 400 ° C and has a special hole for the excess steam. In addition, the transparent cover allows you to always follow the process of cooking.

Using advanced technology and high quality materials made it possible to create a new pattern of kitchen utensils - high-quality, functional, cost-effective with respect to your time and energy, stylish and beautiful - dishes Krauff Feine is designed to ensure your comfort and care for the health of your supply.
